Julius Haan studied at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie in Amsterdam and the AA School of Architecture in London. He has shot for firms including Herzog & de Meuron, Zaha Hadid Architects, and Kengo Kuma & Associates.

His prints are held in museum collections in Amsterdam, Berlin, and Shenzen. Commissions are accepted on a selective basis with lead times of 6–12 weeks.
